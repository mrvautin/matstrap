Material design with Bootstrap

Sometimes you want to switch to Material design without rewriting all your markup. Welcome, Matstrap.

Uses the base syntax of Bootstrap with some small sugar to make give it flexibility of Material design.

See the demo

Getting it

You can run with our color scheme or you can roll your own.

Moving from Bootstrap to Material (Matstrap) is as easy as replacing your existing boostrap.min.css with ours.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="matstrap.min.css" media="screen">
